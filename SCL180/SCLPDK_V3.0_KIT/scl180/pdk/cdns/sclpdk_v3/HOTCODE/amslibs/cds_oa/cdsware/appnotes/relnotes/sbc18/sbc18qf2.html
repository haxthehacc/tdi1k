General:
* This is the initial release of the SBC18QF2 Design Kit.  

Process Summary:
* The SBC18QF2 process is built on the backbone of the SBC18 family
  of processes which is TowerJazz' standard 0.18um SiGe BiCMOS platform. Details
  concerning this process can be found in the following document:
  PS-1090 rev. 3 - Design Application Note for the SBC18QE Family of Processes
* The SBC18QF2 processes has four layers of aluminum metal including
  dual, thick metals for M3 and M4. This process is built on high resistivity 
  substrate with nominal resistivity of 1000  ohms-sqcm. It supports
   (a) 1.8 and 5V CMOS 
   (b) 6V and 8V SiGe HBTs 
   (c) unsilicided poly resistors 
   (d) a 2 fF/um2 MIM capacitor on M2
   (e) inductors using the thick aluminum top metal layers.
* There are four unique features in this process:
   (1) The two SiGe HBTs designed for operation at higher voltages as 
       compared with those available in other SBC18 processes
   (2) The use of dual thick metal layers on the top two metal layers. 
       M3 is 2.8um thick Aluminum in all cases and M4 is 5.2um (QF2) thick Aluminum.
   (3) The use of slotted via3s to connect the thick top metal layers.
   (4) The use of high resistivity starting material

Applicable documents:
* PS-1090 rev. 3 - Design Application Note for the SBC18QE Family of Processes

Device Library:
* Note that the JIT version used for calculating inductor model parameters
  is currently calculating based on 3um thick metal4 used in sbc18qe1.  The
