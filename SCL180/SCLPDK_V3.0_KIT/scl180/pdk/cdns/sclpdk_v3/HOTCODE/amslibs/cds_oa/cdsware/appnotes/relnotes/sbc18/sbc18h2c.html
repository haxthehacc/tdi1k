SBC18H2C Open Access Design Kit Release Notes ________________________ Date: 04/27/2012 Library: sbc18 Relevant Specification Documents: * Design Rules: NPB-PS-0179, Rev. 13 * Electrical Spec: NPB-PS-0267, Rev. 17 * Design Application Note for SBC18H2B: NPB-PS-0542, Rev. 8 Process Synopsis: * The SBC18H2C process has 6 layers of aluminum metal and supports: - 1.8V and 3.3V MOSFETs, - high speed and standard NPNs with deep trench, - poly resistors (silicided, low and high value unsilicided), - N-well resistor, - 2.8fF/um2 MIM capacitor between metal4/metal5 or metal3/metal4, - 5,6fF/um2 stacked MIM capacitor between metal3 and metal5, - MIS capacitor, - schottky diode, - lateral pnp, - square and octagonal inductors using 2.8um thick top metal6, - MOS varactor, - deep trench isolation.
