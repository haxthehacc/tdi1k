SBC18HA Design Kit Release Notes ________________ Date: 2015/03/11 Version 2.0 Library: sbc18 Relevant Specification Documents: * Design Rules: NPB-PS-0179, Rev. 15 * Electrical Spec: NPB-PS-0267, Rev. 17 SBC18HA Process Synopsis: * The SBC18HA process has 6 layers of aluminum metal and supports: - 1.8V and 3.3V MOSFETs, - high speed, standard, and high voltage NPNs with deep trench, - TiN resistor (between metal3/metal4), poly resistors (silicided and low or high value unsilicided), N-well resistor, - 2.8fF/um2 MIM capacitors between metal4/metal5 and metal3/metal4, - square and octagonal inductors using 2.8um thick top metal6, - Schottky diode, - MOS and high performance junction varactors, - triple well isolation. SBC18HX Process Synopsis: * The SBC18HX process has 6 layers of aluminum metal and supports: - 1.8V and 3.3V MOSFETs, - high speed, standard, and high voltage NPNs with deep trench, - TiN resistor (between metal3/metal4), poly resistors (silicided and low value unsilicided), N-well resistor, - 1.0fF/um2 MIM capacitors between metal4/metal5, - square and octagonal inductors using 2.8um thick top metal6, - MOS and high performance junction varactors. SBC18HXL Process Synopsis: * The SBC18HXL process has 6 layers of aluminum metal and supports: - 1.8V and 3.3V MOSFETs, - high speed, standard, and high voltage NPNs with deep trench, - TiN resistor (between metal3/metal4), poly resistors (silicided and low value unsilicided), N-well resistor, - 1.0fF/um2 MIM capacitors between metal4/metal5, - square and octagonal inductors using 2.8um thick top metal6, - Schottky diode, - MOS and high performance junction varactors, - triple well isolation. SBC18HLL Process Synopsis: * The SBC18HLL process has 6 layers of aluminum metal and supports: - 1.8V and 3.3V MOSFETs, - standard and high voltage NPNs with deep trench, - TiN resistor (between metal3/metal4), poly resistors (silicided and low value unsilicided), N-well resistor, - 2.0fF/um2 MIM capacitors between metal4/metal5, - square and octagonal inductors using 2.8um thick top metal6, - Schottky diode, - MOS varactor, - triple well isolation. This is the second major release of the next-generation SBC18 kit. This release replaces the kit released in 2011. For designs created in the legacy 2006 kit, a Generic Design Conversion tool is available from the TowerJazz pull-down menu to aid in the process of migrating legacy designs.
