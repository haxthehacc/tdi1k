DESCRIPTION:

'balun' implements the rectangular spiral transformer. It consists of an interdigitated primary and secondary winding. A center tap splits the secondary winding in two.

ATTRIBUTES:

  • Width of Segment: Controls the width of one balun segment.
  • Space Between Segments: Controls the space between balun segments.
  • Number of Turns: Controls the total number of turns (primary + secondary) in the balun.
  • Xsize: The total width of the structure in the X direction. Xsize and Ysize are constrained to be equal
  • Ysize: The total height of the structure in the Y direction. Xsize and Ysize are constrained to be equal
    • .

NOTES:

LVS:

  • For LVS to recognize the balun, the marking layer must be intersected by 5 metal4 wires that connect to the 5 metal4 pins of the balun. These pins can't be connected to layer other than metal4.
  • The following attributes are checked during LVS:
    • Width, Spacing, Number of Turns, XSize and YSize
