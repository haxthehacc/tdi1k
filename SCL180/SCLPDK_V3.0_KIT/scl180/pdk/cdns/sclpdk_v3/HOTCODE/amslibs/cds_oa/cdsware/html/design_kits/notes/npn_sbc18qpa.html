DESCRIPTION:

'npn' implements the vertical NPN bipolar transistor. The emitter length and number of emitter/base/collector fingers can be controlled. Low and medium breakdown voltage devices are available.

ATTRIBUTES:

  • Multiplier: Controls the number of transistor instances connected in parallel. Specifying a 'Multiplier' of n is equivalent to drawing n fet instances in the schematic and connecting them in parallel. In VirtuosoXL the device will be implemented as n separate layout cells. This attribute can only be used in the schematic
  • Voltage Type: Controls the npn voltage type.
  • Model Type: Controls the model used to simulate the device. 'hicum' selects a hicum model.
  • Emitter Width: Controls the emitter finger width.
  • Emitter Length: Controls the emitter finger length.
  • EmBaseColl Fingers: Controls the number of emitter, base and collector fingers. A device with EBCf='122' will have 1 emitter, 2 base, and 2 collector fingers .
  • Thermal Resistance: Enable/Disable thermal resistance effects in the model. Not available in all processes.
  • Include Metal Parasitics: Enable/Disable metal parasitics in the model. Not available in all processes.
  • Emitter Metal: Controls the type of metal that is used to connect the emitter and collector fingers. M1 uses only metal1. M2 uses a stack of metal1/metal2 for emitter and collector.
  • Temp Rise From Ambient Controls the temp rise value
  • Show Tap Properties Controls the viewing of the tap contact fields
  • Left Sub Contact Controls contact placement on the left side of the ring.
  • Right Sub Contact Controls contact placement on the right side of the ring.
  • Top Sub Contact Controls contact placement on the top side of the ring.
  • Bottom Sub Contact Controls contact placement on the bottom side of the ring.

NOTES:

LVS:

  • The following attributes are checked during LVS: Emitter Width, Emitter Length, Emitter Fingers, Base Fingers, Collector fingers, and Device voltage type
