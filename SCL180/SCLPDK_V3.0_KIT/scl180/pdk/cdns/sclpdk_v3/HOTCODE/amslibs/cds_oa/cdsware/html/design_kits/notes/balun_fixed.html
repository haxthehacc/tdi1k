DESCRIPTION:

'balun' and 'balun_xfmr' implement a fixed-size rectangular spiral transformer. It consists of an interdigitated primary and secondary winding. The balun device has center taps that split the windings in two. The balun_xfmr does not have center taps.

ATTRIBUTES:

  • Inductance: The approximate inductance of the structure. A list of supported sizes is provided.

NOTES:

LVS:

  • For LVS to recognize the balun, the marking layer must be intersected by at least the T1, B1, T2, B2, and CT2 wires in top-metal that connect to the 5 top-metal pins of the balun. The CT1 pin can remain unconnected. It is not allowed to connect the pins in any layer other than top-metal.
  • For LVS to recognize the balun_xfmr, the marking layer must be intersected by all of the T1, B1, T2, and B2 wires in top-metal that connect to the 4 top-metal pins of the balun. It is not allowed to connect the pins in any layer other than top-metal.
  • The following attributes are checked during LVS:

    • Inductance: checked by comparing model name against text label in marking layer.
    Balun vs Balun_xfmr: checked by comparing model name against text label in marking layer.
