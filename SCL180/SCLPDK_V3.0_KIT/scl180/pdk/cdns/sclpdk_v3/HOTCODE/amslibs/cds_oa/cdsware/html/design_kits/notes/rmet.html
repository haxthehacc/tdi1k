DESCRIPTION:

rmet1, rmet2, ..., rmetN implements the Metal resistor formed by the metal layer 1,2, ..., N. The resistor is defined by the resistor block layer.

ATTRIBUTES:

  • Width: Controls the width of the resistor.
  • Length: Controls the length of the resistor.

NOTES:

LVS:

  • The following attributes are checked during LVS: Resistance, Width, Length.
